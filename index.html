Our Story

Welcome to the Railway Club

Welcome to the LMRCA Railway Club, Navigation Road Crossing, Altrincham. We are a social club with roots that run deep into our local community.

The Railway Club was originally a British Rail Staff Association Club for the London Midland Railway Clubs Association. Established over sixty years ago, we are very proud of our railway heritage and the warm, friendly atmosphere that has made us a cornerstone of Altrincham life.

Our Club Secretary, Dennis Whiting, has been a member for many years and has also served as Club Steward. Dennis and the whole team are always happy to welcome new faces through our doors.

Whether you're looking to join as a member, hold a private function, or simply find out more — you're in the right place.

Our Spaces

Facilities & Room Hire

We have a range of rooms to suit any occasion — from large celebrations to smaller, more intimate gatherings. All rooms are available to hire.

Function Room

Our largest space, perfect for weddings, parties, christenings, wakes, and corporate events. Includes a stage, full PA and lighting rig, and access to our well-equipped kitchen. Bring your own caterers and DJ — no preferred suppliers list.

Games Room

Home to darts, pool, snooker, and other classic club games. A great space for casual socialising, team nights out, or a relaxed evening with friends. Available for group hire.

The Lounge

A comfortable, welcoming bar lounge — the perfect spot to relax, catch up with friends over a drink, or enjoy one of our regular club events. Fully stocked bar with your favourite drinks always on hand.

Bowling Green

Our beautiful outdoor bowling green is available to members throughout the season. Whether you're a seasoned bowler or a complete beginner, all are welcome to have a go.

Weddings & Celebrations

Your perfect day, your way

The LMRCA Railway Club has played host to countless weddings since the 1950s — with delighted brides, grooms, and guests at each and every one.

Unlike many venues, we don't tie you to a list of preferred suppliers. When you hire our function room, you have complete freedom:

  • Arrange your own caterers with free use of our kitchen and facilities
  • Decorate the room exactly as you've always imagined
  • Invite your own DJ and/or band, who can use our stage and lighting rig
  • Extended bar hours and your favourite drinks specially stocked
  • A small deposit secures your special day
